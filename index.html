About Health Harmonies

Music's lyrics, rhythm, and melodies can influence emotional well-being, promote physical health in pain management, and have great potential to be useful in non-pharmacological interventions for a wide range of conditions. As research continues to explore its therapeutic potential, music is increasingly recognized not only as a form of entertainment but also as a valuable tool in clinical and public health settings to enhance overall quality of life.

Whether you have an exam in 10 minutes or need support during a stressful moment, this platform adapts to your needs fitting smoothly into daily life.

The Problem

Many healthcare settings leave patients without emotional support. Pain management relies heavily on medication, and clinical systems prioritize efficiency over human connection. Current music-based interventions in healthcare are either clinical programs delivered by certified music therapists or passive listening interventions â€” but both lack personalization and fail to address patients' emotional needs holistically.

Emotional Burnout

Patients and clinicians face stress with few non-clinical outlets.

Over-reliance on Medication

Emotional needs are often overlooked in pain management.

Non-Verbal Struggles

Many patients struggle to express how they feel verbally.

Inaccessible Solutions

Music therapy requires certified therapists and is not always accessible in every healthcare setting.

Our Solution

Health Harmonies offers a web-based platform that provides a personalized music intervention combined with guided journaling to help individuals manage stress and emotional wellbeing. This tool aims to empower patients by offering a self-managed, accessible platform for emotional support through music intervention therapy.

Key Features

1. Mood & Stress Check-In

Individuals can input their emotional state at the beginning of each session. This information helps tailor their music experience based on their current mood.

2. Personalized Music Playlists

The platform generates music recommendations based on user's mood, preferences, language, culture, and previous listening history. These playlists vary in tempo and energy level to best fit emotional needs.

3. Guided Journal Prompts

After each music session, users are encouraged to reflect on how the music made them feel, helping them gain emotional awareness and better manage their emotions. The purpose is to help users process their emotional response and provide a place for non-verbal expression.

4. Anonymous Data Collection

Session data is collected anonymously to improve recommendations and support research, while keeping personal information private.

What We Aim to Achieve

Reduce Stress and Anxiety: By offering personalized music and guided journaling, we expect users to have lower stress levels after the experience and improved emotional regulation.

Increase Emotional Awareness: The journaling encourages patients to reflect on their feelings and helps them to better manage them over time.

Research & Methodology

Background and Significance

The integration of music therapy and guided journaling in healthcare settings has proven to help reduce stress, anxiety, and pain perception, while enhancing emotional well-being. Our platform aims to improve emotional awareness and management by providing a personalized music therapy experience combined with reflective journaling. Music therapy has long been recognized for its emotional benefits, but existing solutions often lack personalization and fail to encourage emotional processing. Health Harmonies addresses these gaps by offering music interventions that adapt to individual emotional states and cultural backgrounds.

Gap Analysis

Current music interventions in healthcare fall into three categories: clinical music therapy, passive listening, and digital music tools. However, they often lack personalization and fail to address patients' emotional needs holistically. Clinical music therapy, while effective, requires certified therapists and is not always accessible in every healthcare setting. Passive listening and many digital music tools tend to be generic and do not foster emotional reflection or non-verbal expression. There is a need for a solution that offers accessible, personalized, and emotionally supportive interventions that integrate seamlessly into healthcare environments.

Study Design

The effectiveness of Health Harmonies will be evaluated through pre-post surveys and user engagement tracking. We measure emotional states (stress, anxiety, and pain) using a 0â€“10 scale before and after platform usage. The survey will also assess the emotional awareness and sense of connection that users feel after their sessions.

Pre-Survey

Measures baseline stress, anxiety, emotional awareness, and connection.

Post-Survey

Evaluates changes in stress, anxiety, pain, and the impact of journaling.

Engagement Tracking: Collects data on session frequency, session length, and features used by participants. This helps identify trends and maximize the efficiency of the platform.

Primary Outcome Measures

Stress Level: Participants will rate their perceived stress and anxiety level on a 0-10 scale before and after each session.

Secondary Outcome Measures

Emotional Awareness: Participants will rate their ability to identify and express emotion using a Likert scale.

Future Research

If possible, we will investigate how the frequency of platform usage influences stress reduction, as well as whether journaling after music listening improves emotional regulation more than music alone.
